# Webshop Requirements

## User Experience
- Intelligent product search, including autocomplete, typo tolerance, and voice search for easy product discovery.
- Mobile-first, responsive design optimized for all devices with touch-friendly interfaces and streamlined mobile checkout.
- Clear product categorization, detailed descriptions, high-quality images, and specifications tailored for electronics.
- User accounts with registration/login, order history, wishlist, and product reviews & ratings to foster transparency.
- Fast loading times (sub-2-second preferred) for smooth browsing and shopping experience.

## Functional Features
- Comprehensive product catalog with categories and filtering options specific to electronics (brands, specifications, price range).
- Shopping cart functionality including add/update/remove products, save for later, and guest checkout.
- Secure and versatile payment integration supporting credit cards, PayPal, and other local/global gateways.
- Order management system with tracking, order history, cancellation, and returns processing.
- Customer support tools such as live chat, FAQs, contact forms, and email notifications.

## Backend and Management
- Inventory management with real-time stock level updates, low stock alerts, and easy restocking workflows.
- Admin dashboard for managing products, orders, customer data, and promotional campaigns.
- Analytics and reporting for sales performance, user behavior, and inventory insights.
- Content management system to handle banners, blogs/news sections, and policy pages (return, shipping, privacy, terms).

## Scalability and Performance
- Support for multilingual interfaces and multi-currency transactions to reach international customers.
